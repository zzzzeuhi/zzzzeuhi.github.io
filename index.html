Eat Pick iOS App Design

EatPick



An iOS app help users picking a restaurant from their favorite lists or nearby restaurants within one-click.



This is a freelance individual design project and developed by other iOS developers.

Inspiration

Deciding where to eat is always a difficult problem for a group of people who are busy with works or study but still keep high-level requirements about meal.

They may have a list of fancy restaurants but it’s sometimes hard and time consuming to pick one from the list for the current meal.
